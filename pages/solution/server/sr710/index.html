SR710
Scalable 2U Rackmount Server

Built for Cloud, Edge, and HPC Workloads

Powered by Dual Intel® Xeon® 6 Scalable processors, SR710 delivers up to 32× DDR5 RDIMM, up to 8× PCIe Gen5 CXL2.0 expansion slots, and up to 24 hot-swap NVMe bays in an OCP DC-MHS modular 2U platform for demanding data center and edge workloads.

Dual Intel® Xeon® 6 32× DDR5 RDIMM 8× PCIe Gen5 / CXL2.0 24× NVMe OCP DC-MHS · 2U

Key Features

Powered by Intel® Xeon® 6 Processors

Utilizes the latest Intel® Xeon® processors, providing unmatched processing power ideal for intensive applications like HPC, AI workloads, and data analytics. The multi-core architecture ensures maximum processing efficiency, meeting the demands of data-intensive applications.

Modular DC-MHS Architecture

OCP-ready design allows seamless upgrades and flexible integration with modular components, enabling rapid scaling in diverse environments. Built for data centers looking to future-proof their infrastructure with open and adaptable standards.

Flexible Expansion with PCIe Gen5 & OCP NIC 3.0

Offers up to 8× PCIe Gen5 slots, including options for dual 25GbE, to enable high-speed networking and connectivity. SR710's compatibility with Senao's SX904 SmartNIC enhances low-latency and high-throughput performance, ideal for SDN, cloud, and virtualized workloads.

Advanced Security and Management

Features secure boot, IPMI, and Redfish for robust remote management, allowing secure monitoring and configuration. Built-in security protections guard data integrity, making SR710 suitable for sensitive, compliance-focused deployments.

Efficient Power & Cooling

Equipped with Senao 80 PLUS Titanium power supplies for minimized power usage and operational costs. Advanced cooling system ensures consistent, reliable performance under sustained workloads.
